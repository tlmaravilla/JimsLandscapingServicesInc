About Us

Jim's Landscape Services Inc. was founded and is owned and operated by Jim and Theresa Camerden since 2000. For over TWO decades we have created many of Colorado Springs and surrounding areas finest landscapes. We would be honored to design and install your landscape projects. We are committed to providing quality work at a fair price.

Our Promise: We will do the following for you

  • 1. Provide quality work at a fair price
  • 2. Create an extended living space outdoors for you and your family to enjoy
  • 3. Assist you in getting that project done you don't seem to have the time to complete
  • 4. Renovate your existing yard or create a new landscape for the first time
  • 5. Any project and always glad to help your project in phases, to fit your budget/timeline
Accomplishments
